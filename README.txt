Aigostar brings your smart bulbs into Homey. Switch them on or off, dim them, set a warm or cool white, and pick any color, straight from Homey or from your Flows.

Sign in once with your AigoSmart account and your bulbs are added automatically, so you can control the lighting in every room and weave it into your automations, from a gentle morning glow to a bright working light or a colorful evening scene.
